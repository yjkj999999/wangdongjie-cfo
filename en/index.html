About

Executive Profile

From EY auditor to Group CFO — 26 years driving financial strategy and capital excellence

Wang Dongjie is a seasoned strategic finance executive with AICPA, CGMA, CFA certifications and a Bachelor's degree in Financial Management from Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (211/ Double First-Class University). English working proficiency.

Career began at Ernst & Young Shanghai (1999–2003), followed by Suntory (China) as Project Manager, Goodbaby International as Group Audit Director (witnessed PAG's landmark LBO and HKEX IPO), Beyond Construction as CAE, Zhaodi Group as Group CFO, Sunny Energy as Group CFO (led Eraya Power's STAR Market spin-off IPO), and currently CFO at Sunvin Group overseeing a ¥1.5B new materials industrial park and Nasdaq listing preparation.

Served companies ranging from ¥380M to ¥20B in revenue. Managed 70+ JV investments (CTG, SPIC). Led ¥400M NEV M&A integration. Built 92-person finance team with "Strategic Finance-BP-Shared Services" three-pillar model. Mentor at CEIBS CFO Association, keynote speaker at ACCA Summit, lecturer at Shanghai International Studies University.
